Magdi Yacoub Heart Foundation (MYF)

  • Health
  • 0 Comments

The Magdi Yacoub Heart Foundation (MYF) was founded in 2008 by renowned Egyptian cardiac surgeon Sir Magdi Yacoub. The foundation's headquarters are located in Aswan, Egypt, with a focus on providing high-quality cardiovascular healthcare services, education, and research. MYF's primary mission is to improve cardiac care in Egypt and the wider region by offering advanced medical treatments, raising awareness about cardiovascular diseases, and conducting research to advance medical knowledge in the field. One of MYF's notable achievements is the establishment of the Aswan Heart Centre (AHC), a state-of-the-art medical facility dedicated to cardiac care. The AHC provides comprehensive services, including diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation, to patients with heart conditions, regardless of their ability to pay. Additionally, MYF is committed to training the next generation of healthcare professionals through its educational programs. It offers training opportunities for physicians, nurses, and other healthcare professionals to enhance their skills and expertise in cardiac care. Furthermore, MYF has played a significant role in changing the landscape of healthcare in Egypt by making advanced cardiac treatments accessible to a wider population. Through its initiatives, MYF has helped thousands of Egyptians receive life-saving treatments and improve their quality of life. Looking ahead, MYF continues to expand its efforts to reach more underserved communities in Egypt and the region. The foundation aims to further enhance its services, promote preventive measures against cardiovascular diseases, and contribute to medical research to find innovative solutions for heart-related conditions.

Founders :

The Magdi Yacoub Heart Foundation (MYF) was founded by Sir Magdi Yacoub, a renowned Egyptian cardiac surgeon, in 2008. Dr. Yacoub is widely recognized for his contributions to cardiac surgery and his dedication to improving healthcare in Egypt and beyond. Sir Magdi Yacoub is a distinguished Egyptian cardiac surgeon, widely celebrated for his groundbreaking contributions to the field of cardiothoracic surgery. Dr. Yacoub's contributions to medicine and philanthropy have earned him numerous accolades, including knighthoods from both the British and Egyptian governments. His unwavering commitment to advancing cardiac care and his dedication to serving humanity have solidified his status as one of the world's most revered medical professionals and humanitarians.
