Chris Michaels is an extraordinary visionary, an educator whose passion for teaching knows no bounds. Micheals is 26 years old Born and raised in a small town, he embarked on his educational journey with fervor, earning a Bachelor's degree in Education with honors from a brown university. Fueled by his love for learning and a desire to make a difference, Chris continued his studies, obtaining a Master's degree in Educational Leadership. His educational journey not only equipped him with knowledge but also instilled in him a deep sense of responsibility to uplift and empower others. Chris's unwavering commitment to pro bono teaching for mathematics stems from his belief that education is the key to unlocking opportunities and transforming lives. His classroom is a beacon of inspiration, where students are not just taught, but nurtured, encouraged, and celebrated for their unique abilities. For Chris Michaels, being a teacher is not merely a profession; it is a calling—a calling he embraces wholeheartedly with boundless love and dedication
