Resala Charity Organization

Resala Charity Organization is one of Egypt's largest and most prominent non-profit organizations. Founded in 1999, Resala is dedicated to providing various social services and assistance to underprivileged communities across Egypt. The organization operates through a network of volunteers and branches across the country, offering a wide range of programs and initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and social welfare. Resala's activities include providing food aid, clothing, and shelter to those in need, as well as organizing educational programs, vocational training, and healthcare services for marginalized individuals and families.

Founders :

Resala Charity Organization was founded by a group of young Egyptian volunteers in 1999. While specific names of the founders may not be widely publicized, the organization was established with the shared vision and dedication of these individuals to make a positive impact on society by providing social services and assistance to those in need.
